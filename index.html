B.C. History

Founded in 2014, Buddy Critiques has served as a preimere podcast for listeners all over the globe. Their unrelenting devotion to film critiqing has paved the way for others in their space. The commitment to their craft has helped them climb to the iTunes top 100 list several times. Backed by an award winning team, Buddy Critiques is well equipped to weather any storm.

What is the criteria for guests?

  1. You must be an avid film connoisseur
  2. You have a sense of humor, but by no means are you the funniest in the room
  3. Apply to be a guest today!
